Rename Tabs `tabPosition` property to `tabPlacement` to match antd v6 API.

**Migration:**

- `tabPosition: top` → `tabPlacement: top`
- `tabPosition: bottom` → `tabPlacement: bottom`
- `tabPosition: left` → `tabPlacement: left`
- `tabPosition: right` → `tabPlacement: right`
